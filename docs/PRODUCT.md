# Cognora AI product brief

Cognora AI is a personal learning operating system. It connects every planning, learning, practice, and feedback interaction to one student knowledge profile so that the whole product becomes more useful over time.

## The nine connected capabilities

1. **Study Planner** — creates and adapts a realistic daily and weekly plan.
2. **Lecture Companion** — turns notes, slides, PDFs, and transcripts into structured learning material.
3. **Learning Roadmap Generator** — converts a goal into prerequisites, milestones, checkpoints, and dates.
4. **Assignment Feedback Tool** — reviews work against requirements and turns feedback into an improvement plan.
5. **Knowledge Gap Detector** — maintains an explainable concept mastery model from real evidence.
6. **AI Study Coach** — answers what to do next, explains why, and helps the student recover when plans change.
7. **Practice and Recall Engine** — produces quizzes, flashcards, short answers, and spaced review evidence.
8. **Unified Course Library** — keeps every lecture, material, assignment, concept, and assessment in one course context.
9. **Student Progress Dashboard** — shows today’s priorities, progress, deadlines, risks, and recent insights.

## Product loop

Materials create a course knowledge map. The map creates a roadmap. The roadmap informs the planner. Lectures and practice generate mastery evidence. Assignment feedback exposes gaps. Those gaps update the planner and roadmap. The AI coach explains and coordinates the loop.

## Product principles

- One platform and one shared data model, not a suite of disconnected tools.
- AI proposes; deterministic application rules protect dates, permissions, and critical calculations.
- Every mastery score must show the evidence behind it.
- Student material is private by default.
- AI feedback is advisory and should not be presented as an official grade.
- Major schedule changes require student approval.
